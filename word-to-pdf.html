How to Convert Word to PDF

Converting Word documents to PDF is essential when you need to:

  • Ensure document formatting stays consistent across devices
  • Prepare files for professional printing
  • Share documents that can't be easily edited
  • Create archive copies of important documents

Step-by-Step Guide

1. Upload your Word file - We support both .DOC and .DOCX formats

2. Set conversion options - Choose PDF quality and output name

3. Convert to PDF - Process takes just seconds

4. Download your PDF - Get perfect formatting every time

Pro Tips

  • Use "High quality" for documents with complex formatting
  • "Medium quality" works best for most documents
  • Check margins and page breaks after conversion
  • For large documents, consider splitting into multiple files

Frequently Asked Questions

What Word formats do you support?

We support all modern Word formats including .DOC (Word 97-2003) and .DOCX (Word 2007 and later).

Will my formatting be preserved?

Yes! Our converter maintains fonts, images, tables, and layout exactly as in your original document.

Is there a file size limit?

You can convert files up to 50MB for free. Our premium version handles files up to 200MB.

How secure is my document?

All files are automatically deleted from our servers after 1 hour. We never store or access your documents after processing.

Can I convert PDF back to Word?

Yes! Try our PDF to Word tool for editable document conversion.
