How to Rotate PDF Pages

Rotating PDF pages is essential when you need to:

  • Fix incorrectly scanned documents
  • Adjust portrait/landscape orientation
  • Prepare documents for proper viewing
  • Standardize page orientations in a multi-page document

Step-by-Step Guide

1. Upload your PDF file - We support all standard PDF documents

2. Select pages to rotate - Choose all pages or specific page ranges

3. Set rotation direction - 90° clockwise, 180°, or 90° counter-clockwise

4. Rotate and download - Process takes just seconds for most documents

Pro Tips

  • Use "Rotate all pages" for uniformly oriented documents
  • For mixed documents, rotate specific pages using page ranges (e.g. 1, 3-5, 7)
  • Preview the changes before finalizing the rotation
  • Multiple rotations can be combined (e.g. two 90° rotations = 180° rotation)

Frequently Asked Questions

Will rotating pages affect my PDF quality?

No, rotating pages is a lossless operation that doesn't reduce the quality of your PDF content.

Can I rotate different pages different amounts?

Yes! First rotate one set of pages, then upload the result and rotate other pages as needed.

Is there a page limit for rotation?

You can rotate PDFs with up to 100 pages in the free version. Premium accounts handle up to 500 pages.

Can I undo a rotation if I make a mistake?

Yes, simply rotate the pages back to their original position, or start over with your original file.

Can I rotate password-protected PDFs?

No, we cannot process encrypted or password-protected PDF files for security reasons.
