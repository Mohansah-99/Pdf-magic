How to Convert JPG to PDF

Converting JPG images to PDF is essential when you need to:

  • Combine multiple photos into a single document
  • Create professional portfolios or photo albums
  • Send images in a universally readable format
  • Archive important photos with metadata

Step-by-Step Guide

1. Upload your JPG files - Select one or multiple images to convert

2. Set PDF options - Choose page size, orientation and whether to merge

3. Convert to PDF - Process takes just seconds for most images

4. Download your PDF - Get high-quality PDF with your images

Pro Tips

  • Use "Auto" page size to preserve original image dimensions
  • Enable "Optimize PDF size" for faster sharing
  • For photo albums, merge all images into one PDF
  • Check image order before converting

Frequently Asked Questions

What image formats can I convert to PDF?

We support JPG and JPEG formats. For other image types like PNG or GIF, try our Image to PDF converter.

Will the image quality be preserved?

Yes! By default we maintain the original image quality. You can optionally optimize the file size if needed.

How many images can I merge into one PDF?

You can merge up to 50 images in one PDF with our free version. Premium accounts can merge up to 200 images.

Can I rearrange the order of images?

Yes! Simply drag and drop images in the file list before converting to set your preferred order.

Can I convert PDF back to JPG?

Yes! Try our PDF to JPG tool to extract images from PDF files.
