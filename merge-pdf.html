How to Merge PDF Files Online

Merging PDF documents is essential when you need to:

  • Combine multiple files into one document
  • Create a portfolio or report from different sources
  • Organize scanned documents in sequence
  • Prepare documents for sharing or printing

Step-by-Step Guide

1. Upload your PDFs - Add multiple files at once (drag & drop supported)

2. Reorder files - Drag to arrange them in your preferred sequence

3. Set options - Choose output filename and page settings

4. Merge PDFs - Download your combined document instantly

Pro Tips

  • Use descriptive filenames before merging for easier organization
  • Check page orientation if combining portrait and landscape documents
  • For large files, try our Compress PDF tool first

Frequently Asked Questions

How many PDFs can I merge at once?

You can merge up to 20 PDF files in a single operation. For larger batches, consider splitting them into multiple merges.

Will the merged PDF have watermarks?

No! Our tool creates watermark-free documents. What you merge is exactly what you get.

Is there a maximum file size limit?

You can merge files up to 100MB each. For larger files, try our premium version with no size limits.

How are my files protected?

All uploaded files are automatically deleted from our servers after 1 hour. We never store or access your documents after processing.

Can I reorder pages after merging?

Yes! Use our Split PDF tool to extract pages and merge them again in your preferred order.
