How to Compress PDF Files Online

Compressing PDF documents is essential when you need to:

  • Optimize documents for web uploads
  • Save storage space on your device
  • Speed up document sharing

Frequently Asked Questions

Will compressing reduce my PDF quality?

Our tool uses smart compression that minimizes quality loss. The "Low" setting preserves nearly all quality while still reducing file size.

What's the maximum file size I can compress?

You can compress files up to 100MB for free. Our premium version handles files up to 1GB.

How much can I reduce my PDF size?

Reduction depends on content. Image-heavy PDFs can shrink 50-70%, while text documents typically reduce 20-40%.

Is this tool secure for sensitive documents?

Yes! All files are automatically deleted from our servers after 1 hour. We never store or access your documents after processing.

Can I compress multiple PDFs at once?

Our free version processes one file at a time. Try our Batch PDF Tools for multiple files.
